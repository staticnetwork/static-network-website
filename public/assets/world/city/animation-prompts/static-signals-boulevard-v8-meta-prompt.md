# STATIC Signals Boulevard V8 Meta Animation Prompt

## Prompt

Animate this as a real-life handheld night video from a flagship phone or mirrorless camera, not a game render and not concept art. Keep the original composition, buildings, signs, street layout, STATIC logos, and overall identity intact.

Make the scene feel like a chaotic real opening night on STATIC Signals Boulevard after rain. Add subtle but believable motion:

- people walking, filming, laughing, waiting in line, and crossing the wet street
- foreground phones shifting slightly as people record
- luxury cars inching forward with headlights and brake lights reflecting in puddles
- security and valet staff waving people and cars through
- camera flashes and camera crews moving through the crowd
- LED billboards flickering, changing brightness, and playing short social-feed loops
- rooftop guests moving on balconies
- drone lights drifting slowly above the street
- steam and rain mist moving through the boulevard
- reflections rippling lightly across wet pavement

Camera motion: very slow handheld push-in with tiny natural sway, as if someone is walking into the crowd. Keep it premium and realistic, not shaky chaos.

Mood: loud, expensive, crowded, cinematic, real, overwhelming, and exciting. The viewer should feel physically present inside STATIC City.

Avoid warping buildings, changing faces too much, changing STATIC logos, inventing real-world brands, melting signage, adding fantasy creatures, turning it into a game render, or making the camera fly unnaturally.

## Recommended Settings

- Duration: 5-8 seconds
- Aspect: 16:9
- Motion strength: medium-low to medium
- Camera motion: slow push-in / handheld drift
- Style: real-life night event video

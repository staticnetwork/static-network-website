# STATIC Companion / Guardian Prompts

Use these prompts for prototype generation in Meshy, Tripo, Rodin, or other 3D asset tools. Keep every asset original, commercial-safe, and free of real-world brands, copied game/film IP, celebrity likenesses, hate symbols, or tactical instruction.

## Guardian Bodyguard

```text
Create an original premium guardian companion for STATIC Network, a futuristic entertainment city. Full-body fictional guardian bodyguard, tall powerful silhouette, protective but elegant, black/violet/champagne color palette with subtle neon green companion-system accents, luxury cyber-nightlife armor styling, expressive hands, readable face or helmet, high-status bodyguard energy, suitable to walk beside a player Entity in an open-world social city. Game-ready GLB, rig-ready humanoid or creature-humanoid, neutral A-pose or T-pose, clean topology, separated material zones, cinematic 8K concept quality but optimized for web export. No copied IP, no celebrity likeness, no real weapon branding, no real military logos.
```

Negative prompt:

```text
No gore, no ripped game assets, no Marvel, no DC, no Fortnite, no GTA, no Ready Player One copies, no real-world gang symbols, no hate symbols, no realistic tactical instruction, no extra limbs, no broken hands, no unreadable face, no baked text, no low-resolution textures.
```

## Acceptance Checklist

- File opens in a GLB viewer.
- License is commercial-safe or clearly acceptable for internal prototype use.
- First web version is preferably under 10 MB for companions and under 20 MB for venues.
- Model is original and does not copy a known creature, robot, brand, celebrity, game, or film.
- Materials read well under dark neon lighting.
- Large companions have scale variants or a clear plan for LOD reduction.
- The asset can be replaced without changing route logic.

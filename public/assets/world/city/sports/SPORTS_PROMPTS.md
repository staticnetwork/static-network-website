These prompts are for internal asset generation/sourcing only. Keep everything original and commercial-safe. Do not use NBA, NCAA, real team names, real athlete likenesses, real sponsors, or copied sports-game assets.

## Acceptance Checklist

- The venue reads as STATIC, not a generic arena.
- Basketball is clear without using any real league/team/IP.
- The asset can export as GLB for web preview.
- The source can later be rebuilt or upgraded for Unreal Engine.
- Screens/signage are separate enough to become dynamic billboards later.
- Crowd pieces are modular, not one huge expensive mesh.

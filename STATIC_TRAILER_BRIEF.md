# STATIC City Public Trailer Brief

Internal owner direction for the public landing-page trailer slot.

## Purpose

The Arrival District should open immediately. The trailer should play inside the district as a premium screen, not block the visitor before they can use the page.

## Current Source Clips

- `/assets/videos/static-arrival-walk-01.mov`
- `/assets/videos/static-arrival-walk-02.mov`

These are now treated as trailer source scenes, not the full trailer.

## Trailer Goal

Create a cinematic first-look trailer for STATIC City that sells the world in 20-45 seconds:

- Water-surrounded STATIC City core.
- Arrival District at night with billboards, crowds, cars, clubs, and creator venues.
- Signal Walk / red carpet / creator venue row.
- STATIC Radio rooftop.
- STATIC PLAY portal district.
- Market Walk.
- Arena Sports Quarter.
- Wondercore immersive district.
- STATIC Island and yacht arrival.
- The Fields macro concept: Desert, Forest, Snow, and player-owned land.
- Prompt-based creation inside the world: music studio, game board, gallery, marketplace, venue builder.
- Underground tunnel glimpse: Rootside service corridors, Gophur couriers,
  hidden routes, package delivery, and shortcut energy beneath the luxury city.

## Direction

Photoreal, high-end, cinematic, luxury nightlife, open-world launch-trailer energy. It should feel like a real place filmed at night, not a generic game mockup.

When the final trailer is ready, place it in:

`public/assets/videos/static-city-trailer.mp4`

Then update the Arrival District trailer console to prefer that final trailer while keeping the two current clips as source-scene alternates.

# STATIC Story Mode Roadmap

STATIC should eventually have a cinematic open-world story mode: GTA-scale ambition, better-feeling controls, more realistic graphics, and a more original social/creator backbone.

The campaign should not be built before the city systems exist. Build the systems, then let story mode make those systems feel legendary.

## Target Feel

- Cinematic open-world missions.
- Third-person walking with strong controller feel.
- Luxury SUV driving and later full vehicle handling.
- Vibe Mode first-person cruising.
- Enterable hero venues.
- NPC crowds and named characters.
- Companion/bodyguard presence.
- Shopping, wardrobe, jewelry, property, Radio, Signals, events, and creator tools as mission mechanics.
- Realistic UE5.8 lighting, MetaHuman-level character fidelity, and high-end animation.

## Phase 1: Origin Missions

These are tutorial only in function, not in feel. They should feel like a premium opening chapter.

1. Arrival and city orientation.
2. Entity creation/claim.
3. First fit at Market Walk.
4. First ride with valet and Radio.
5. First venue: Radio Rooftop, Club STATIC, Arena, or PLAY.
6. First prompt station.
7. First property/spawn preview.
8. First hidden discovery after age-gating/moderation exists.

## Phase 2: First Campaign Arcs

- Mr. Stone founder chapters.
- Rising Entity chapters.
- Crew/faction chapters.
- Racing and takeover chapters.
- Sports/arena chapters.
- Creator/studio chapters.
- Island/yacht chapters.
- Hidden-nightlife chapters.
- City mystery chapters.

## Mission Rule

Every story mission must unlock, teach, or stress-test a real system:

- movement
- vehicle handling
- interiors
- NPC presence
- companions
- inventory
- Signals
- Radio
- properties
- events
- creator tools
- moderation/access rules

No filler missions just to say story mode exists.

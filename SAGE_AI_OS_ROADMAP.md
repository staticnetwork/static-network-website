# S.A.G.E. AI OS Roadmap

S.A.G.E. is STATIC’s AI operating layer: concierge, guide, operator, and creative copilot.

## Product layers

- 3D arrival and persistent holographic presence
- Browser voice with British Executive default
- Opt-in higher-quality ElevenLabs voice
- Push-to-talk conversation and future opt-in “Hey S.A.G.E.” wake phrase
- Entity creation through conversation and structured Entity DNA
- Provider activation guidance and secure status checks
- Approved AI image, video, and voice generation
- Signal drafting, posting workflows, and creator scheduling
- Studio, Channel, World, Drop, Live, and Marketplace assistance
- User-controlled memory, preferences, routines, and next-best-action guidance
- Future browser navigation and external automation through permissioned tools
- Provider switching and a future API marketplace

## Control model

Every capability passes through four layers: understand, preview, approve, execute. Sensitive operations never skip preview and approval. The activity log records suggestions, route changes, confirmations, and completed actions without storing voice audio by default.

The current build implements local understanding, route control, Entity/Signal prefills, confirmations, activity history, browser speech, a 3D procedural stand-in, and secure provider boundaries. Production S.A.G.E. GLB assets, paid providers, continuous wake-word detection, external browser tools, and autonomous routines remain future work.
